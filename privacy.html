Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how MCM LLC Services (“MCM,” “we,” “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website or use our Smart Booking Assistant.

Last updated January 3, 2026
If you have questions, contact us at booking@mcmllcservices.com.

1) Overview

We collect information to provide and improve our services, respond to requests, and deliver booking and lead notifications. We do not sell your personal information.

2) Information We Collect

Information you provide
  • Contact details (such as name, email, phone) when you submit a request or booking inquiry
  • Service selections, preferred time windows, and messages you provide
  • Business owner contact details used to receive lead notifications (email/SMS), when enabled
Information collected automatically
  • Basic usage data like pages viewed, approximate referrer, and device/browser information
  • Cookies or similar technologies for analytics and site functionality (where enabled)
  • Event logging for product performance (e.g., “booking attempt” events)

3) How We Use Information

  • Provide requested services, respond to inquiries, and facilitate scheduling
  • Send transactional notifications to business owners (e.g., new lead or urgent request alerts)
  • Improve and secure the Smart Booking Assistant and related systems
  • Measure performance and troubleshoot issues (analytics and event logs)

4) SMS Notifications (CTIA Disclosures)

If SMS notifications are enabled, messages are transactional and related to lead/booking activity (not marketing).

  • Message Frequency: Varies. Typical volume is 0–5 messages per day depending on activity.
  • Opt-In: Business owners opt in during onboarding by providing a phone number and enabling SMS notifications.
  • Opt-Out: Reply STOP to opt out at any time.
  • Help: Reply HELP for assistance.
  • Rates: Message and data rates may apply.

Note: End customers who schedule through a third-party calendar provider (e.g., Calendly) are subject to that provider’s data collection policies and terms.

5) Sharing of Information

We may share information with service providers only as needed to operate the system, such as:

  • Email/SMS delivery providers (for transactional notifications)
  • Hosting and infrastructure providers
  • Analytics providers (where enabled)

We do not sell personal information. We may disclose information if required to comply with law or protect our rights.

6) Data Retention

We retain lead and event data only as long as needed for business operations, reporting, and legal/compliance needs. We may delete or anonymize data periodically.

7) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8) Your Choices

  • SMS: Reply STOP to opt out; reply HELP for assistance.
  • Email: Business owners can disable notifications in their configuration or by contacting us.
  • Cookies: You can control cookies via your browser settings (analytics may be reduced).

9) Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above reflects the most recent revision.
